Who Am I?

Landing onto this page there's quite a high chance you want to look at my work. That's pretty cool! But let's put that to the side for now and break the ice a wee bit.

Well to start, if you haven't caught it earlier, Elaine Mae Villarino is the name of this lil ol' person. On long quiet walks by myself, I would always end up contemplating about my sense of self. Hoping I would come up with a clear-cut answer to sum myself up; though it is quite simpler if I were to describe myself in with this sentence. With my open-mindedness, willpower, individuality, imagination and strategic abilities I am and will be capable of doing anything I have set my mind to.

But for now, I'm a student in her third semester of the Software Engineering Technology - Interactive Gaming program at Centennial College, Scarborough Ontario. And previously, I've studied at Marshall McLuhan Catholic Secondary School, Toronto Ontario; where I focused on the Digital Arts i.e. Animation, Film, Graphic Design and Web Design.
